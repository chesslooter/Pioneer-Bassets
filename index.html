Who We Are

We are a family-based, basset hound home located in Southeastern Wisconsin. We have been raising, training, showing, competing and having fun with our basset hounds in multiple activities for many years. We uphold the breed standard and are an American Kennel Club Breeder of Merit. When we breed our bassets, we do so selectively and infrequently, to promote healthy puppies with a loving, friendly temperament and high-quality structure.
